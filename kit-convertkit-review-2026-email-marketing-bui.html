Common questions

How is Kit different from Mailchimp?
Kit is built specifically for creators, not marketing teams. It uses a subscriber-centric model (no duplicates), has simpler automations, and includes built-in commerce for selling digital products. Mailchimp is more general-purpose but more complex.
Is the free plan good enough?
Kit's free plan supports up to 10,000 subscribers with landing pages, opt-in forms, and broadcasts. Paid plans ($25/mo+) unlock automations, sequences, and advanced commerce features.
Can I sell products with Kit?
Yes. Kit has native commerce features for selling digital downloads, courses, coaching, and subscriptions. Payments via Stripe — no separate checkout tool needed.
What is the Creator Network?
It's a free growth feature where your content gets recommended to other creators' audiences (and vice versa). Think of it as organic cross-promotion baked into the platform.
