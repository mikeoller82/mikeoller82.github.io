// and who's better off with bulk manufacturing

TapStitch is not for brands selling 1,000+ units per month of the same design. If you've validated demand, know your sizes, and have predictable volume — bulk manufacturing will always be cheaper per unit. That's the honest truth. POD's per-unit cost premium doesn't make sense when inventory risk is minimal.

TapStitch is for the testing phase, the launch phase, and the long tail. It's for the brand that wants to try 20 designs instead of betting on 3. It's for the creator launching merch without a warehouse. It's for the designer who knows their art will sell but doesn't have $5,000 to prove it. Zero inventory risk means the worst-case scenario is zero sales at zero cost — not $10,000 of hoodies gathering dust.
