👥 Who It's For

// anyone spending money on ads who needs to prove ROI

Most ad platforms tell you how many clicks you got and what your cost-per-click was. That's useful for benchmarking, but it doesn't answer the question that actually matters: "Did this campaign make me money?" Diginius closes that gap by connecting ad platform data with actual transaction data from your store or CRM.

The platform is especially valuable for businesses where the path from click to purchase spans multiple sessions, channels, or days. When a customer clicks a Google Ad on Monday, browses your site, leaves, comes back Wednesday from an organic search, and then buys — most analytics tools credit the organic search. Diginius attributes the sale back to the original paid click that started the journey.

  • E-commerce stores running Google/Microsoft Ads — See revenue per campaign, per keyword, per ad group. Identify your most profitable products and the ads that sell them. Kill underperforming campaigns before they drain another month's budget. Real data, not modeled estimates.
  • Marketing agencies managing client ad spend — Prove ROI with revenue data, not engagement metrics. Client retention improves dramatically when you can show "your $5K ad spend generated $18K in revenue" instead of "you got 200K impressions." White-label reports close renewals.
  • DTC brands scaling paid acquisition — Know your true customer acquisition cost by factoring in actual order values. Scale campaigns that drive profitable customers. Identify which product categories respond best to paid search. Data-driven scaling instead of budget guessing.
  • PPC professionals & consultants — Move beyond click optimization to revenue optimization. Differentiate your services with profit-based campaign management. Deliver insights that cheaper tools can't provide because they lack the revenue connection.
