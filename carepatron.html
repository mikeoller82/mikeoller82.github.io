// built for healthcare providers of all sizes

Carepatron is designed for healthcare providers who want to modernize their practice without the complexity and cost of traditional EHR systems. Whether you're a solo practitioner or a multi-provider clinic, Carepatron scales with your needs while keeping operations simple.
