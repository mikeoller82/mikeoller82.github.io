Common questions

Is Printify really free?
Yes. The free plan lets you create unlimited designs and connect up to 5 stores. You only pay for products when they're ordered by customers. A Premium plan ($29/mo) offers product discounts of up to 20%.
How's the print quality?
Quality depends on the print provider you choose. Printify shows ratings and reviews for each provider. Order samples before going live to ensure the quality meets your standards.
Does Printify handle returns?
Printify handles returns for defective or misprinted items. For other returns, it depends on your store's return policy — Printify works with your existing process.
What platforms can I sell on?
Shopify, Etsy, WooCommerce, Wix, Squarespace, eBay, and PrestaShop. You can also use the Printify API for custom integrations.
