Common questions

Is QuillBot free?
Yes. The free plan includes paraphrasing (125 words at a time), grammar checking, and summarizing. Premium ($9.95/month) unlocks unlimited paraphrasing, all modes, plagiarism detection, and faster processing.
Will it make my writing sound like AI?
No. QuillBot rewrites your words — it doesn't generate content from scratch. Your voice and ideas stay intact, just expressed more clearly. Most readers can't tell anything was edited.
Does it work with Google Docs?
Yes. QuillBot has extensions for Google Docs, Microsoft Word, and Chrome. Use it directly in any writing environment without switching tabs.
Is it good for non-native English speakers?
Absolutely. QuillBot is especially popular with non-native speakers who want to write natural-sounding English. The "Simple" mode is perfect for clarity, while "Formal" works for academic and professional writing.
