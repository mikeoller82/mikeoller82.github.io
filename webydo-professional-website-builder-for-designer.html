Common questions

How is Webydo different from Webflow?
Webydo is more focused on visual design freedom with a traditional canvas editor (think Photoshop for websites). Webflow is more developer-oriented with a box-model approach. Webydo's CMS and white-label features are also more agency-friendly.
Do I need to know code?
No. Webydo's canvas editor handles all the HTML/CSS generation. You design visually and the clean code is generated automatically. Advanced users can access custom code if they want.
Can clients edit content without breaking the design?
Yes. Webydo's CMS lets clients update text, images, and blog posts through a locked-down editor. They can't accidentally move elements or break your layout.
Is white-labeling included?
Agency plans include full white-labeling — custom domain, your branding on the dashboard, and client-facing portals under your agency name.
