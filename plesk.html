👥 Who It's For

// built for web professionals who value their time

Plesk is designed for anyone who manages web servers but doesn't want to spend their life in the command line. Whether you're hosting client websites, running a development agency, or managing your own infrastructure, Plesk provides the GUI-based control panel that makes server administration accessible and efficient.

The platform is particularly valuable for web professionals who manage multiple servers or client sites. Instead of remembering dozens of commands and config file locations, Plesk puts everything in one searchable, intuitive dashboard that saves time and reduces errors.

  • Web Hosting Providers — offer managed hosting to clients with a professional control panel they can access themselves
  • Digital Agencies — manage dozens of client websites and WordPress installations from one dashboard

  • IT Teams — standardize server management across teams with role-based access and audit logs
