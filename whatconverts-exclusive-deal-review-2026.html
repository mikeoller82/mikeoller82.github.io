🎯 Who This Is Actually For

// and who's wasting their money without it

WhatConverts is not for businesses running pure e-commerce with online checkout only. If your entire customer journey happens on your website — visit, add to cart, checkout — Google Analytics already tracks that. WhatConverts is for businesses where the conversion happens offline: a phone call, an in-person visit, a consultation booked through a form. If your customers call you before they buy, you need this.

Every business spending $1,000+/month on Google Ads, Microsoft Ads, or any paid traffic source without call-level attribution is guessing which ads work. "We got 47 leads this month" means nothing if you can't answer "which keywords generated the 12 that actually became customers?" That gap between leads and revenue is where budgets go to die.

  • Marketing agencies managing client ad spend — Stop reporting on impressions and clicks. Start reporting on revenue per campaign. Client retention jumps when they can see "your $5K ad spend drove $22K in revenue" instead of "you got 200K impressions." WhatConverts turns your reports from vanity metrics into business proof.
  • Service businesses running PPC (plumbers, lawyers, dentists, HVAC) — Your customers call before they buy. If you can't trace that call back to the exact keyword and ad that generated it, you're optimizing blind. WhatConverts closes the loop between click and revenue so you can kill underperforming keywords and double down on winners.
  • Multi-location businesses — Track leads by location, by campaign, by channel. Know which location's ads are performing and which are bleeding budget. Centralized reporting across all locations without manual aggregation.
