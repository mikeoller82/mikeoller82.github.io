Common questions

Is PartnerStack only for B2B SaaS?
PartnerStack specializes in B2B SaaS and digital products, but it also works for e-commerce and services. The platform is purpose-built for recurring revenue models.
How does the partner marketplace work?
Over 80K active partners browse the marketplace looking for new programs to promote. When you list your program, partners can discover and apply to join. It's like a recruiting channel for your affiliate program.
Can I handle payouts through PartnerStack?
Yes. PartnerStack handles the entire payout process — calculating commissions, generating invoices, and paying partners via PayPal, direct deposit, or check. You fund one account, they handle the rest.
What does it cost?
Pricing is based on partner-generated revenue. Plans start at a platform fee plus a percentage of partner payouts. Contact their sales team for a custom quote based on your program size.
