What Are Capsule and Transpond?

Capsule is a simple, intuitive CRM designed specifically for small and medium businesses. Trusted by thousands of teams worldwide, it provides everything you need to manage contacts, track sales opportunities, and organize customer relationships—without the bloat and complexity of enterprise CRM systems.

Transpond is Capsule's sister email marketing platform, designed to integrate seamlessly with your CRM data. Together, they form a complete customer management and marketing automation system: Capsule handles your contacts and sales pipeline while Transpond manages email campaigns, automated sequences, and marketing communications.

The combination is powerful because your CRM and email marketing share the same data. When a contact moves through your sales pipeline, Transpond can automatically trigger relevant email sequences. When someone engages with an email campaign, that activity appears in their Capsule contact record. This tight integration eliminates the data silos that plague businesses using separate CRM and email tools.

Capsule CRM: Contact Management That Just Works

Capsule's contact management is designed for humans, not data scientists. Every contact record shows the complete history—emails, notes, files, tasks, and sales opportunities—in one chronological view. Auto-enriched profiles pull in social network information automatically, giving you context before every conversation.

The sales pipeline uses a visual Kanban board where you drag opportunities from stage to stage as deals progress. Track potential values, forecast revenue, and see conversion rates at a glance. The pipeline dashboard provides a single view of your entire sales process without requiring complex setup or training.

Task management integrates directly with your contacts and opportunities. Create tasks for follow-ups, meetings, and deadlines that link to specific deals. Repeating tasks and automated reminders ensure nothing falls through the cracks. Calendar views and personal calendar subscriptions keep your schedule synchronized.

Transpond: Email Marketing That Syncs With Your CRM

Transpond handles email marketing automation with triggers and sequences that respond to contact behavior and pipeline changes. Create welcome sequences for new contacts, nurture campaigns for leads in specific pipeline stages, and re-engagement campaigns for dormant customers—all automatically triggered by actions in your CRM.

The marketing automation uses Capsule's contact data for precise targeting. Send different campaigns to contacts based on tags, pipeline stage, purchase history, or any custom fields you've defined. This CRM-native targeting produces higher engagement rates because every email is contextually relevant to the recipient's relationship with your business.

Template management and shared inboxes keep your team's communications consistent and professional. Connect Gmail or Outlook to send directly from Capsule, with all correspondence automatically logged to the relevant contact record.

Sales Pipeline and Reporting

Capsule's sales pipeline goes beyond basic deal tracking. Create multiple pipelines for different products, services, or markets. The visual Kanban interface makes it intuitive to see where every opportunity stands and identify bottlenecks in your sales process.

Reporting covers sales analytics, activity tracking, and pipeline performance. Monitor conversion rates, average time to close, and won/lost ratios. Advanced reports on the Growth plan provide deeper insights into sales cycle efficiency and team performance. All reports support filtering by time period, user, or team, and data can be exported for further analysis.
