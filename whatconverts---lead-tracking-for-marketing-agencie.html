Common questions

How does call tracking work?
WhatConverts uses dynamic number insertion — visitors from different sources see different phone numbers. When they call, the system knows exactly which ad, keyword, and landing page generated that lead.

Yes. WhatConverts is built for agencies. Manage unlimited clients from one dashboard, with separate tracking pools and white-label reports for each.

Yes. WhatConverts feeds conversion data back to Google Ads automatically. Optimize campaigns based on actual leads, not just clicks and form fills.
