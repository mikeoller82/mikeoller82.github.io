What is QuillBot?

QuillBot is an AI-powered writing assistant that uses advanced natural language processing to help users improve their writing. Originally launched as a paraphrasing tool, QuillBot has evolved into a comprehensive writing suite that includes grammar checking, summarization, citation generation, plagiarism detection, and even translation. It serves millions of users worldwide—from students polishing essays to professionals crafting business communications.

The platform's core strength lies in its ability to understand context and produce paraphrased text that maintains the original meaning while improving readability, style, and flow. In 2026, QuillBot remains one of the most accessible AI writing tools, requiring no technical expertise to use effectively.

QuillBot's paraphrasing engine consistently produces natural-sounding rewrites that preserve semantic meaning—a critical capability for students and professionals alike.

Key Features

  • Paraphrasing Tool: Rewrite sentences, paragraphs, or entire documents with multiple modes (Standard, Fluency, Creative, Concise, Formal, Academic, etc.)
  • Grammar & Spell Checker: Advanced proofreading with explanations for corrections and style suggestions
  • Summarizer: Condense long articles, papers, or documents into concise summaries while retaining key points
  • Citation Generator: Automatically create properly formatted citations in APA, MLA, Chicago, Harvard, and other styles
  • Plagiarism Checker: Scan text against billions of web pages and academic papers to identify potential plagiarism
  • Co-Writer: AI-assisted writing suggestions, completions, and expansions for various content types
  • Translator: Multi-language translation with AI refinement for natural output in the target language
  • Word Counter: Accurate word, character, and sentence counting for meeting submission requirements

  • Social Media Managers: Repurpose content across platforms by adjusting tone and length while maintaining core message

Whether you're a student trying to improve your grades, a professional aiming for clearer communication, or a content creator managing multiple projects, QuillBot offers tools that can streamline your writing process.

Pricing

QuillBot offers several pricing tiers to accommodate different usage patterns and budgets:

  • Free Plan: Paraphrase up to 125 words per input (unlimited times), grammar check, summarizer (up to 1,200 characters per summary), 3 synonym suggestions, standard paraphrasing modes, basic word count
  • Premium Monthly ($19.95/month): Unlimited paraphrasing with no word count limits, access to all paraphrasing modes (including Creative, Fluency, Academic), advanced grammar checker, plagiarism checker (20 pages/month), faster processing speed, priority support
  • Premium Annual ($99.95/year): All premium features with ~40% savings compared to monthly billing; best value for committed users
  • Team/Business Plans: Custom pricing for teams; includes centralized billing, admin controls, user management, and volume discounts

The free plan is surprisingly robust for casual users, providing enough daily usage to evaluate whether premium features are needed. Students on a budget can often get significant discounts through educational pricing programs.

Verdict

Final Recommendation: Start with the free tier to experience QuillBot's capabilities. For most serious writers, QuillBot is a worthwhile addition to the toolkit—and one that you'll likely use daily.
