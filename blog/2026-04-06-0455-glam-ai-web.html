THE "VIRAL CONTENT" MYTH

Glam AI's main pitch is that their AI creates content that trends. Their site says "Transform your photos into captivating AI videos with Glam.AI's powerful AI model. Create viral content with trending effects like AI Kiss, Hug, Muscle."

But here's the problem: anyone can slap an AI filter on a photo and call it "viral." The actual "viral" part has nothing to do with the app — it has to do with your existing following, timing, and luck. The app generates an image. It doesn't generate a viral strategy.

One Reddit user in r/MakeupAddiction noted: "It's an AI image and video generator specifically built for creators who need to pump out trend-ready content without spending hours on complex..." That's closer to truth. The app helps you generate content faster. That's not the same as going viral.
