What is PartnerStack?

PartnerStack is the infrastructure behind most of the affiliate and partner programs you encounter in the B2B SaaS world. If you've ever signed up to promote a tool like Tidio, Trainual, Kartra, or Descript through their "partner program," there's a strong chance PartnerStack was the platform powering that relationship — handling your tracking links, commission calculations, payouts, and dashboard.

Founded in Toronto, PartnerStack has positioned itself as the go-to partner ecosystem platform for SaaS companies that want to run affiliate, referral, and reseller programs without building the infrastructure themselves. Over 400 B2B companies use the platform, collectively driving hundreds of millions in partner-sourced revenue. For affiliates and partners, it provides a single dashboard to discover, join, and manage programs across dozens of SaaS products.

In 2026, PartnerStack has expanded its marketplace significantly, making it easier for affiliates to discover high-quality programs with recurring commission structures. The platform now features real-time analytics, automated payouts, and deep integrations with CRMs, payment processors, and marketing tools — making it one of the most complete partner management ecosystems available.

Key Features

PartnerStack serves two distinct audiences — the companies running programs and the partners promoting them. Here's what matters for affiliates and partners:

  • PartnerStack Marketplace: A curated directory of 400+ B2B SaaS affiliate programs searchable by category, commission structure, and payout terms. Discover new programs to promote without hunting through individual websites. Filter by recurring vs. one-time commissions, cookie duration, and average deal size.
  • Single Partner Dashboard: Manage all your PartnerStack-powered programs from one login. Track clicks, conversions, commissions, and payouts across every program you've joined. No more juggling 15 different affiliate dashboards with 15 different interfaces.
  • Recurring Commission Tracking: Unlike traditional affiliate networks that pay once per sale, many PartnerStack programs offer monthly recurring commissions — you earn every month the customer stays subscribed. The platform tracks multi-month payouts automatically, including upgrades, downgrades, and churn adjustments.
  • Automated Payouts: Commissions are calculated and paid automatically on a regular schedule (typically monthly). Direct deposit, PayPal, or wire transfer options. Detailed payout reports show exactly which referrals generated which commissions — no spreadsheet reconciliation needed.
  • Real-Time Analytics: Live tracking of clicks, sign-ups, trials, conversions, and revenue. Attribution data shows which content, channels, and campaigns drive the most valuable referrals. Optimize your promotion strategy based on actual conversion data, not guesses.
  • Co-Marketing Resources: Access to branded assets, landing page templates, email swipe files, product demos, and sales enablement materials provided by each program. Many companies offer partner-exclusive webinars, early feature access, and co-marketing campaigns.
  • CRM & Tool Integrations: Connects with Salesforce, HubSpot, Stripe, and other business tools. API access for custom integrations. Zapier support for workflow automation. Partners can pipe their referral data into existing reporting systems.
  • Tiered Partner Programs: Many programs on PartnerStack offer tiered rewards — the more you refer, the higher your commission rate, the better your terms, and the more exclusive perks you unlock. Silver, Gold, and Platinum tiers with escalating benefits.

Who Is PartnerStack For?

PartnerStack's audience breaks into two clear groups, and this review focuses on the partner/affiliate side:

Affiliate marketers and content creators who review, recommend, or create content about SaaS tools. If you run a blog, YouTube channel, newsletter, or social media presence covering business software, PartnerStack's marketplace gives you access to dozens of high-paying programs from a single dashboard. The recurring commission model means your content compounds — a review you write today can generate income for months or years.

Agencies and consultants who recommend tools to clients can layer affiliate income on top of their consulting fees. When you recommend a CRM, email tool, or training platform to a client, signing up through your PartnerStack link means ongoing commission revenue while you're providing the service. It turns every client recommendation into a passive income stream.

B2B influencers and newsletter operators with engaged professional audiences find PartnerStack programs particularly lucrative. B2B SaaS commissions tend to be higher than consumer affiliate programs because the products themselves are more expensive. A single conversion on a $99/month tool with 30% recurring commission generates $30/month in passive income — from one referral.

The shift from one-time affiliate payouts to recurring commissions is the most important change in B2B affiliate marketing in the last decade. PartnerStack is the platform making that shift possible at scale.

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Recurring commission model means long-tail income — one referral can pay for months or years
  • Single dashboard manages all PartnerStack-powered programs — no login fatigue
  • Marketplace makes discovery easy — find new programs to promote without hunting individually
  • Automated payouts with transparent reporting — no chasing payments or reconciling spreadsheets
  • High-quality SaaS programs with proven products — lower refund rates than digital product affiliate networks
  • Tiered programs reward top performers with higher commissions and exclusive perks
  • Real-time analytics help optimize which content and channels drive the best conversions

Cons

  • Program approval isn't instant — some companies vet partners before accepting applications
  • Commission rates vary wildly between programs — some are generous, others modest
  • Payout minimums on some programs mean small affiliates may wait for thresholds
  • Platform is B2B SaaS focused — not useful for consumer product, physical goods, or course affiliates
  • Recurring commissions depend on customer retention — if your referral churns, income stops

Pricing

PartnerStack is free for partners and affiliates. There is no cost to join the marketplace, sign up for programs, or receive commissions. The companies running programs pay PartnerStack a platform fee — partners pay nothing.

This is an important distinction from some affiliate networks that charge membership fees or take a percentage of partner commissions. On PartnerStack, every dollar of commission the program offers goes directly to the partner. The company absorbs the platform cost as part of running their partner program.

For companies running programs, PartnerStack's pricing starts at approximately $800/month for the basic plan, scaling up based on partner count and features. But for affiliates and partners reading this review — the cost is zero. Sign up, apply to programs, and start earning.

How PartnerStack Compares

Against Impact.com, PartnerStack is more focused on B2B SaaS while Impact covers a broader range including retail, travel, and consumer brands. Impact has more enterprise features; PartnerStack is more accessible for individual affiliates and smaller creators.

Against ShareASale and CJ Affiliate, PartnerStack's key advantage is the recurring commission model. Traditional networks mostly offer one-time payouts. PartnerStack programs typically pay monthly for the lifetime of the customer — dramatically changing the economics for affiliates who create durable content like reviews and comparisons.

Against individual affiliate programs run on platforms like FirstPromoter or Rewardful, PartnerStack offers the advantage of consolidation. Instead of managing 20 separate logins, tracking systems, and payout schedules, everything runs through one dashboard. The tradeoff is that not every SaaS company uses PartnerStack — but the 400+ program marketplace covers most of the major B2B tools.
