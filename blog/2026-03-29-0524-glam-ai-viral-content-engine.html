What I'd Change

Nothing is perfect. A few things I'd want to see improve:

Creative control limitations. The template-first approach is the product's strength and its ceiling. Power users will eventually bump up against the boundaries. Expanding customization options within templates — not removing them — would help retain advanced creators.

Transparency on models. Knowing which models power the generation would help serious users evaluate quality and reliability. "All major models" is marketing copy. Technical users want specifics.

Mobile-first, desktop-lagging. The product is clearly optimized for mobile creators. Desktop users might find the experience less polished. Given that most social content starts on phones, this is the right bet — but a stronger desktop interface would capture the professional market too.
