What is Descript?

Descript is an AI-powered video and audio editing platform that has fundamentally changed how creators approach media production. Instead of navigating complex timelines and scrubbing through hours of footage, Descript lets you edit video and audio the same way you'd edit a document—by editing text. Transcribe your media, then simply delete words to remove corresponding video segments, rearrange paragraphs to reorder scenes, or type new dialogue to generate it with AI voice cloning.

The platform's AI assistant, "Underlord," automates tedious production tasks that traditionally consume hours: removing filler words (ums, uhs), cleaning up background noise, generating captions, creating highlight reels, and even producing synthetic voiceovers that match your voice. For creators who need to produce more content in less time, Descript represents a paradigm shift in what's possible without professional editing skills.

Founded in 2017, Descript has grown from a simple transcription tool into a comprehensive content creation suite. It now serves millions of users—from solo podcasters to enterprise marketing teams—and continues to push boundaries in AI-assisted media production.

Descript doesn't just make editing faster—it makes editing accessible to people who have never touched a traditional video editor.

Key Features

  • Text-Based Video Editing: Edit video by editing a transcript—delete words to remove footage, drag paragraphs to reorder scenes, search to find moments instantly
  • AI Filler Word Removal: Automatically detect and remove "um," "uh," "you know," and other filler words with one click
  • Studio Sound: AI-powered audio enhancement that removes background noise, evens out volume, and makes recordings sound studio-quality
  • AI Voice Cloning (Overdub): Create a synthetic version of your voice to correct mistakes or generate new narration without re-recording
  • Automatic Transcription: Fast, accurate speech-to-text in 25+ languages with speaker identification
  • Screen Recording & Multitrack: Record screen, webcam, and microphone with separate tracks for professional-quality output
  • AI Captions & Subtitles: Generate animated captions and subtitles automatically with customizable styles
  • Collaboration Tools: Share projects, leave comments, and edit together in real-time with team members
  • Direct Publishing: Export directly to YouTube, social media platforms, podcast hosts, and more

Pricing

Descript offers tiered pricing for different usage levels:

  • Free: 3 transcription hours/month, 1 hour of remote recording, basic AI features, watermarked video exports
  • Creator ($12/month): 10 transcription hours/month, unlimited remote recording, 4K exports without watermark, all basic AI features
  • Pro ($24/month): 30 transcription hours/month, advanced AI features (Overdub, Studio Sound), unlimited stock library, filler word removal
  • Business ($40/month): 40 transcription hours/month, full AI suite, team collaboration, dedicated account manager, priority support
  • Enterprise: Custom pricing, unlimited usage, SSO, audit logs, custom integrations, SLA guarantees

The Free plan is generous enough to test the core workflow. Most serious creators find the Creator or Pro plans offer the best value for regular content production.
