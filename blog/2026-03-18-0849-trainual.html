Where Trainual Falls Short

No review is honest without the downsides. Here's what I found:

  • Content creation takes effort. The AI helps, but if your processes aren't documented at all, the initial setup is a project. Plan for 2-4 weeks of real work to build out your content library.
  • It's not cheap for small teams. If you're under 10 people and rarely hire, the ROI math gets fuzzy. This platform is built for companies scaling headcount or managing distributed teams.
  • Mobile experience is fine, not great. It works on phones, but the training modules are clearly designed for desktop. Not a dealbreaker, but worth noting if your workforce is mostly mobile.
  • Integration ecosystem is growing but not deep. It connects with the major HRIS platforms, but if you're using a niche toolset, check compatibility before committing.
