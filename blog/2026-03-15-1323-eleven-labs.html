What is Eleven Labs?

Key Features

Eleven Labs covers the full spectrum of AI voice applications:

  • Text-to-Speech with Emotional Range: Convert any text into natural speech across 30+ languages (expanding to 70+). The v3 model understands context, emphasis, and emotional tone from the text itself — no markup or SSML tags needed. Voices range from warm and conversational to authoritative and documentary-style.
  • Instant Voice Cloning: Upload 30-60 seconds of clean audio and Eleven Labs creates a usable clone in under a minute. The cloned voice maintains the original speaker's tone, cadence, and character. Use it to generate any script in that voice without re-recording. Accuracy improves with longer source samples.
  • Voice Library: Thousands of pre-made voices across languages, accents, ages, and styles. Browse by category — narration, conversational, documentary, character — or search by voice attributes. Each voice can be used commercially on paid plans.
  • AI Dubbing & Translation: Translate any video into 30+ languages while preserving the original speaker's voice, emotional tone, and timing. The dubbed output maintains lip-sync approximation and natural pacing. One source video becomes a multilingual content library.
  • Sound Effects Generator: Generate custom sound effects from text descriptions — "rain on a tin roof," "crowded restaurant ambiance," "sci-fi laser blast." Useful for video editors and podcast producers who need ambient audio without licensing stock libraries.
  • Conversational AI Agents: Build always-on AI voice agents for customer support, bookings, and real-time conversations. Low-latency response times enable natural back-and-forth dialogue. Integrates with knowledge bases and CRM systems.
  • API & SDK: Full-featured API for programmatic access to all features. SDKs for Python, JavaScript, and Swift. Webhook support for real-time processing. Rate limits are generous on paid plans, enabling high-volume production pipelines.
  • Projects (Long-Form Editor): A workspace for creating audiobooks, podcasts, and long-form content. Import scripts, assign voices to characters, adjust pacing per paragraph, and export chapter-by-chapter. Designed for producers who need more control than single-clip generation.

Pricing

Eleven Labs uses a character-based pricing model across four tiers:

  • Pro — $99/month: 500,000 characters, 160 custom voices, 44.1kHz PCM audio output, usage analytics, higher API limits. For teams and high-volume production.
  • Scale — $330/month: 2M characters, all Pro features, lowest latency, priority support. For businesses running production pipelines or voice agents.

How Eleven Labs Compares

Against PlayHT, Eleven Labs produces noticeably more natural-sounding output, especially on longer passages. PlayHT offers more voices in its library, but Eleven Labs wins on the voices that matter — the ones listeners actually want to hear.

Against Google Cloud Text-to-Speech and Amazon Polly, those services offer lower per-character pricing but sound robotic by comparison. They're fine for system notifications and accessibility features. For consumer-facing content, Eleven Labs is in a different quality tier entirely.

Against Descript's Overdub, the tools serve different purposes. Overdub is designed for correcting and editing recorded audio within the Descript editor. Eleven Labs is a standalone voice generation platform for creating audio from scratch. They complement each other more than they compete.
