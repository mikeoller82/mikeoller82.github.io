What is Refresh.me?

Refresh.me is an all-in-one digital wellness and habit-tracking platform that combines AI-powered coaching with evidence-based behavioral science to help users build lasting habits, optimize productivity, and improve overall well-being. Unlike simple habit trackers that only log completion, Refresh.me provides personalized insights, adaptive goal setting, and real-time guidance to keep users motivated and on track.

The platform integrates various wellness dimensions—including mindfulness, exercise, nutrition, sleep, and productivity—into a single unified dashboard. Using AI algorithms, Refresh.me learns from user patterns, identifies potential obstacles, and suggests tailored interventions. It's like having a personal wellness coach available 24/7, without the high cost of human coaching.

What sets Refresh.me apart is its ability to adapt to individual rhythms and provide contextual nudges that actually change behavior—not just track it.

Pricing

Refresh.me offers a freemium model with tiered subscription options:

  • Free Plan: Basic habit tracking (up to 5 habits), standard reminders, basic progress stats, community access, limited integrations
  • Premium Monthly ($14.99/month): Unlimited habits, AI coaching with personalized insights, advanced analytics (correlations, trends), priority support, all guided programs, full integration suite, custom themes
  • Premium Annual ($99.99/year): All premium features with ~40% savings; includes early access to new features
  • Family Plan ($24.99/month): Up to 5 family members each with premium features, shared family challenges, unified billing

The free plan is substantial enough to experience the core tracking functionality and decide if the premium features justify the cost. For most serious users, the annual plan offers the best value.
