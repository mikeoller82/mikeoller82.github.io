Deliverability is mixed. One Sender.net review noted: "The harshest critics call the platform unreliable for deliverability." That's a real concern for high-volume senders. If you're sending 50k+ emails daily, you might see throttling or lower inbox rates.

Support quality has slipped. One Trustpilot review from mid-2025 reads: "Looks like things started going to hell in mid 2025, and were completely boogered up by 2026. It's all AI-run now." Another StartupOwl review noted: "Negative reviews describe support skirting around issues, slow account recovery." That's concerning if you need help.

The rebrand from ConvertKit to Kit confused everyone. Existing links, documentation, and brand recognition got disrupted. One user on Reddit wrote about exporting data from kit.co which is shutting down. The transition hasn't been seamless for everyone.

One G2 summary captures it: "Positive reviews on Trustpilot and G2 mention fast response times and helpful agents." But the negative reviews cite slow support and throttling issues. Mixed bag.
