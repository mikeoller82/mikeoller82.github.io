🎯 Who Actually Needs This

// and who's wasting their time without it

Diginius is not for brand awareness campaigns where the goal is impressions and reach. If you're running display ads to get your logo in front of eyeballs and you don't care about direct attribution, Google's native reporting handles that fine. Diginius is for performance marketers and businesses that need to answer one question: "Did this ad make me money?" If you can't connect ad spend to revenue, you need this tool.

The businesses bleeding the most from attribution gaps are the ones spending $2,000+/month on Google Ads or Microsoft Ads while tracking conversions as form fills or page views. A form fill is not a sale. A page view is definitely not a sale. Until you connect ad clicks to actual orders in your e-commerce platform, your "optimization" is just rearranging budget based on guesses.

  • E-commerce brands running Google/Microsoft Ads — Stop trusting Google's modeled conversion values. Connect your actual order data and see revenue per campaign, per keyword, per ad group. Kill the campaigns that drive window shoppers. Scale the ones that drive buyers.
  • Agencies managing client PPC spend — Client retention dies when you report on clicks and impressions. It thrives when you report "your $5K ad spend generated $18K in revenue from these specific campaigns." Revenue attribution is the report that renews contracts.
  • DTC brands scaling paid acquisition — Know your true customer acquisition cost by connecting ad spend to actual order values. Factor in margins to see which campaigns are profitable at scale and which look good on paper but lose money in practice.
