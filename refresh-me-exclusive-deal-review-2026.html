🎯 Who Actually Needs This

// and who's fine without it

Refresh.me is not a budgeting app. If you need zero-based budgeting with envelope categories, spending limits per category, and alerts when you're about to overspend — use YNAB. Refresh.me is a visibility tool. It shows you where your money is and where it went. It doesn't tell you where it should go. That distinction matters because the people who benefit most are the ones who don't want another complicated system — they want answers without effort.

If you're currently "tracking" finances by checking your bank app when you remember, glancing at your credit card balance when the statement comes, and occasionally opening a spreadsheet you haven't updated in three weeks — Refresh.me replaces all of that with a single 30-second daily check.
