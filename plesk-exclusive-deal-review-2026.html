❓ Frequently Asked Questions

// the stuff people actually want to know

Is Plesk worth it for less than 10 sites?

Probably not. If you manage under 10 sites, manual management or a simpler tool may be more cost-effective. Plesk starts showing ROI around 15-20 sites, where the time saved on updates, security, and monitoring exceeds the per-server license cost. Once you hit 30+ sites, it's a no-brainer.

Can I use Plesk with my existing hosting provider?

Yes. Plesk runs on virtually any VPS or dedicated server from any provider — AWS, DigitalOcean, Vultr, Hetzner, Linode, or your own hardware. It installs on top of your server's OS and manages everything from there. No migration needed.

Does Plesk replace cPanel?

Yes, Plesk is a direct alternative to cPanel. Both are web hosting control panels with similar core functionality. Plesk differentiates with its WordPress Toolkit, cleaner multi-site management, and broader OS support (Windows + Linux, while cPanel is Linux-only).

What happens if I stop paying for Plesk?

Your sites keep running — Plesk doesn't host anything, it manages servers that already exist. If your license lapses, the management panel stops working, but your websites, databases, and email continue functioning. You'd just lose the automation and dashboard until you renew.

Is there a free trial?

Yes, Plesk offers a 14-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start. You can test it on a live server with real sites before committing to a license.
