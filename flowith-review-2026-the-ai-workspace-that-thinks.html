Flowith supports multiple leading models including GPT-4, Claude, Gemini, and others. You can switch models per conversation and even compare outputs side by side.

Yes. Share canvases with team members, collaborate in real time, and assign AI agent tasks to specific projects. Perfect for research teams, creative agencies, and product squads.
